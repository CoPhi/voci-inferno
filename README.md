# Voci dall'Inferno Repository 
**Voci dall’Inferno** is a research project coordinated by *Marina Riccucci* (professor of the Department of Philology, Literature, and Linguistics at the University of Pisa). 

The project integrates research and teaching, aiming to build a digital environment for the collection, analysis, and preservation of *Holocaust testimonies*. 

The project focuses on the creation of the first structured digital corpus of non-literary testimonies in italian by concentration camp survivors.

The project aims to analyze and investigate the presence of *Dantean lexicon* within the archive's digitized testimonies.
